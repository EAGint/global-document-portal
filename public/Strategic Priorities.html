Strategic Priorities 2026–2030

Scaling structured delivery across fragile and emerging markets.

Strategic Context

Between 2026 and 2030, EAGint will consolidate its position as an integrated infrastructure and logistics delivery platform operating across Somalia, East Africa, and selected fragile markets. Our strategy focuses on disciplined growth, institutional partnerships, and governance-driven expansion.

Priority 1

Deepen Infrastructure & Public Asset Delivery

  • • Expand marine & port infrastructure
  • • Scale public works (roads, utilities)
  • • Strengthen PPP delivery architecture
Target Outcome: Preferred partner for co-financed public infrastructure.
Priority 2

Expand Renewable Energy & Climate Infrastructure

  • • Develop solar and hybrid mini-grids
  • • Integrate battery storage systems
  • • Align projects with climate finance
Target Outcome: Climate-aligned provider contributing to SDG 7 & 13.
Priority 3

Institutional Procurement & Framework Growth

  • • Expand Long-Term Agreements (LTAs)
  • • Strengthen vendor-managed inventory
  • • Improve cross-border sourcing
Target Outcome: Increased recurring institutional framework contracts.
Priority 4

Operational Footprint Expansion

  • • Strengthen regional logistics corridors
  • • Expand warehousing capabilities
  • • Increase multi-country capacity
Target Outcome: Enhanced geographic resilience and reduced vulnerability.
Priority 5

Governance & Impact Strengthening

  • • Maintain ISO-aligned systems
  • • Expand impact measurement metrics
  • • Integrate gender inclusion frameworks
Target Outcome: Full Tier-1 institutional credibility.
