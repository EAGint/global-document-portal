Partnerships & PPP

Trusted Collaboration • Shared Risk • Long-Term Impact

Partnership Philosophy

EL ALI GROUP INTERNATIONAL LTD (EAGint) believes sustainable impact in complex environments is achieved through structured partnerships that combine public mandate, private-sector efficiency, and community engagement.

We collaborate with United Nations agencies, international NGOs, governments, donors, and private-sector partners to deliver infrastructure, logistics, humanitarian, and development solutions under strong governance and ESG frameworks.

Who We Partner With

Multilateral & UN System

EAGint aligns its operations with UN procurement, compliance, and safeguarding standards, including eligibility through UNGM Level 2 verification (Vendor ID 262897), enabling structured engagement with UN entities and affiliated agencies.

International & National NGOs

  • Implementing partner roles
  • Logistics and supply-chain enablers
  • Infrastructure and services providers

Partnerships are governed by MoUs, safeguarding commitments, and donor-aligned reporting requirements.

Governments & Public Institutions

  • Infrastructure and public works delivery
  • Logistics and supply-chain systems
  • Capacity building and service provision

All engagements respect national regulations, public procurement rules, and transparency standards.

Private Sector & Consortia

  • Joint ventures and consortia
  • Specialized subcontracting
  • PPP and blended-finance arrangements

These structured arrangements enable intelligent risk sharing, innovation, and global scale.
