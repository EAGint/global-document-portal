Frequently Asked Questions

Are you UN registered?

Yes, EAGint is a fully registered vendor with the UNGM (United Nations Global Marketplace) and holds active vendor IDs with major agencies.

Do you meet donor compliance standards?

Absolutely. We maintain ISO certifications and strictly adhere to safeguarding, anti-corruption, and PSEA (Protection from Sexual Exploitation and Abuse) policies.

Where do you operate?

Our headquarters are in Somalia, with extensive operations across East Africa and established supply chain corridors globally.
