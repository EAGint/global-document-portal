Board Charter

EL ALI GROUP INTERNATIONAL LTD (EAGint)

Effective Date: 2026
Approved By: Board of Directors
Review Cycle: Every 2 Years

1. Purpose of the Board

The Board of Directors is the highest governing authority of EL ALI GROUP INTERNATIONAL LTD (EAGint). The Board provides strategic direction, governance oversight, risk supervision, ESG and safeguarding accountability, and executive performance oversight. Operational management remains the responsibility of executive leadership.

2. Board Authority

  • Approval of corporate strategy and long-term roadmap
  • Oversight of major capital allocation and PPP participation
  • Infrastructure asset acquisition approval
  • Executive appointment and removal authority
  • Risk tolerance and compliance oversight

3. Board Composition

  • Minimum 5 Directors | Maximum 11 Directors
  • Maximum 3 Executive Directors
  • Minimum 2 Independent Non-Executive Directors
  • At least one Financial / Audit expert
  • At least one ESG / Compliance expert

4. Strategic Oversight Responsibilities

  • Approval of multi-year strategy
  • Annual business plan review
  • Revenue diversification monitoring
  • Vision 2060 alignment supervision

5. Financial Oversight

  • Approval of annual budget
  • Quarterly financial review
  • External auditor appointment
  • Internal control oversight
  • Capital exposure monitoring

6. Risk Management

  • Enterprise risk register review
  • Fragile context exposure supervision
  • Political and security risk oversight
  • Approval of high-risk PPP agreements

7. ESG & Safeguarding Oversight

  • Zero tolerance SEAH enforcement
  • Environmental compliance integration
  • Whistleblowing independence
  • Grievance redress monitoring
  • Safeguarding escalation to Board Chair

8. Governance & Ethics

  • Code of Conduct approval
  • Conflict of interest disclosure review
  • Anti-corruption oversight
  • Transparent procurement supervision

9. Board Committees

The Board delegates specialized oversight to the following dedicated committees:

Audit & Risk Committee

  • Oversees financial integrity and procurement transparency.
  • Reviews enterprise risk framework and exposure thresholds.
  • Supervises internal and external audit processes.
  • Monitors anti-fraud and anti-corruption systems.
  • Reports quarterly to the Board.

ESG & Safeguarding Committee

  • Ensures environmental and social safeguard compliance.
  • Oversees SEAH and child safeguarding systems.
  • Monitors grievance redress mechanisms.
  • Reviews annual ESG and SDG reporting.
  • Maintains zero-tolerance misconduct oversight.

Governance & Ethics Oversight

  • Reviews conflict of interest disclosures.
  • Supervises whistleblowing and ethics cases.
  • Ensures policy updates and governance maturity.
  • Manages Board succession planning.

Investment & Infrastructure Oversight

  • Approves PPP participation and asset exposure.
  • Reviews climate-aligned infrastructure investments.
  • Evaluates concession agreements and risk allocation.
  • Ensures disciplined capital deployment.

10. Board Meetings

  • Minimum four (4) meetings per year
  • Quorum: 50% + 1 Directors
  • Formal minutes recorded and archived

11. Director Duties

  • Act in good faith
  • Exercise independent judgment
  • Avoid conflicts of interest
  • Maintain confidentiality
  • Prioritize long-term institutional interest

12. Chair of the Board

  • Leads meetings
  • Ensures independent oversight
  • Acts as escalation authority
  • Chair & CEO roles to be separated by 2035

13. CEO Relationship

The CEO reports directly to the Board and implements approved strategy. Quarterly operational reporting is mandatory. Annual CEO performance evaluation required.

14. Succession Planning

  • CEO succession plan
  • Executive leadership pipeline
  • Board renewal planning

15. Performance Evaluation

The Board conducts annual self-assessment covering strategy, financial oversight, ESG supervision, and independence integrity. Independent external review recommended every five years.

16. Compliance Commitment

The Board commits to operating in alignment with ISO governance principles, UN and IFI procurement standards, ESG frameworks, and international anti-corruption policies.

17. Long-Term Governance Principle

"Stability over speed. Integrity over expansion. Governance over opportunistic growth. Institutional continuity over short-term gain."

Audit & Risk Committee

The Audit & Risk Committee supports the Board in overseeing financial integrity, enterprise risk exposure, procurement transparency, and compliance systems.

Mandate

  • Review quarterly and annual financial statements
  • Supervise internal and external audit processes
  • Oversee enterprise risk management framework
  • Monitor anti-fraud and anti-corruption systems
  • Approve procurement integrity controls

Authority

  • Access to all financial and operational records
  • Direct reporting from internal audit
  • Independent engagement with external auditors

Meeting Frequency

Minimum quarterly meetings, with additional sessions as required.

ESG & Safeguarding Committee

This committee ensures environmental, social, governance, and safeguarding standards are embedded across all EAGint divisions.

Mandate

  • Oversee ESG policy implementation
  • Monitor environmental compliance
  • Supervise SEAH and safeguarding cases
  • Ensure grievance redress mechanisms function independently
  • Review annual ESG & SDG performance reporting

Safeguarding Oversight

  • Zero tolerance misconduct enforcement
  • Confidential reporting channels supervision
  • Survivor-centered response framework monitoring

Reporting

Annual ESG disclosure summary submitted to the full Board.
