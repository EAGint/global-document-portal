1. Purpose of the Board

The Board of Directors is the highest governing authority of EL ALI GROUP INTERNATIONAL LTD (EAGint). The Board provides strategic direction, governance oversight, risk supervision, ESG and safeguarding accountability, and executive performance oversight. Operational management remains the responsibility of executive leadership, while the Board ensures institutional continuity and fiduciary integrity.

9. Committee Mandates

Standing committees provide specialized oversight on behalf of the Board:

Audit & Risk Committee

  • Oversees financial integrity and procurement transparency across all 13 divisions.
  • Reviews enterprise risk management framework and exposure thresholds in fragile markets.
  • Supervises independent internal and external audit processes.
  • Monitors anti-fraud, anti-corruption, and AML/KYC systems.

ESG & Safeguarding Committee

  • Ensures environmental and social safeguard compliance (ESMF) in IFI/Donor projects.
  • Oversees SEAH and child safeguarding systems and survivor-centered responses.
  • Monitors grievance redress mechanisms (GRM) for operational independence.
  • Reviews annual ESG metrics, carbon reduction, and SDG alignment reports.

17. Long-Term Governance Principle

"Stability over speed. Integrity over expansion. Governance over opportunistic growth. Institutional continuity over short-term gain."
