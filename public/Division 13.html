Education Infrastructure & Facilities

Delivering safe, inclusive, and resilient learning environments that enable access to quality education in fragile, post-conflict, and climate-exposed contexts.

Explore Services

Strategic Role & Value Proposition

Education infrastructure is more than buildings—it is a platform for human capital development and social cohesion. In many contexts, access is constrained by damaged facilities, lack of WASH, and unsafe learning environments.

EAGint addresses these gaps by delivering context-appropriate infrastructure. We combine engineering, critical WASH systems, strict safeguarding, and accessibility to ensure learning spaces are durable, inclusive, and locally owned.

Key Differentiators

  • Extensive experience delivering education infrastructure in fragile settings
  • Integration of WASH, safeguarding, and child-friendly design
  • Strong coordination with local education authorities and NGO partners
  • Scalable delivery from emergency learning spaces to permanent schools
