Institutional FAQ

Knowledge base for partners, donors, and stakeholders.

Registration & Compliance

Are you registered with the United Nations?

Yes. EL ALI GROUP INTERNATIONAL LTD (EAGint) is a registered vendor on the United Nations Global Marketplace (UNGM) at Level 2. This status allows us to participate in high-value procurement across UN agencies globally. Our UNGM ID is 262897.

Governance

Do you meet donor compliance and safeguarding standards?

EAGint operates under ISO-aligned management systems (9001, 14001, 45001) and maintains specific institutional policies for Safeguarding, PSEA, Anti-Corruption, and Whistleblowing. Our framework is designed to satisfy the rigorous audit requirements of international donors and IFIs.

Operations

Where does EAGint operate?

Our primary operational headquarters and nationwide logistics hubs are in Somalia. We maintain regional reach across East Africa and manage global supply chain corridors through our procurement networks in EMEA and Asia.

Capabilities

What sectors does EAGint cover?

Through our 13 specialized divisions, we provide a "one-platform" solution covering Infrastructure & Marine, Logistics & Supply Chain, Renewable Energy, WASH, Healthcare Supply, ICT, and Capacity Development.

Engagement

How can partners engage with EAGint?

We engage through several structured channels:

  • Direct Procurement and Technical Tenders
  • Public-Private Partnership (PPP) concessions
  • Long-Term Agreements (LTA) and Frameworks
  • Consortium participation for multi-sector development programs
