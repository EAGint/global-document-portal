Corporate Policies & Risk Management

Safeguarding & PSEA (Zero Tolerance) ＋
EAGint enforces strict prohibition of sexual exploitation, abuse, and harassment. Safeguarding obligations apply at all times to Board members, employees, contractors, and partners. Survivor-centered approaches and 'Do No Harm' principles dictate our community interactions.
Anti-Fraud & Procurement Integrity ＋
Clear segregation of duties, competitive tendering, and strict conflict of interest declarations. We adhere to the UN Supplier Code of Conduct and actively screen for sanctions and compliance breaches.
Enterprise Risk Management (ERM) ＋
Structured mitigation across four dimensions: Operational (route security, fleet monitoring), Financial (milestone payments, BoQ validation), Environmental (climate impact mapping), and Compliance (audit-ready documentation).
