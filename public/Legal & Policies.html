Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

EL ALI GROUP INTERNATIONAL LTD (EAGint) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting personal data in line with GDPR-aligned principles, proportionate to our operational context.

What Data We Collect

  • Contact information (name, email, organization)
  • Information submitted via contact, careers, or reporting forms
  • Technical data (IP address, browser type, pages visited)

How We Use Data

  • To respond to inquiries and applications
  • To manage partnerships, recruitment, and reporting
  • To improve website performance and security

Data Protection Principles

  • Lawful, fair, and transparent processing
  • Purpose limitation and data minimization
  • Confidentiality and restricted access
  • Secure storage and responsible retention

EAGint does not sell or misuse personal data.

Data Sharing

  • Authorized staff on a need-to-know basis
  • Service providers supporting website operations
  • Authorities where legally required or for security protocols

Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data by contacting our Data Protection Liaison:

privacy@eagint.com
